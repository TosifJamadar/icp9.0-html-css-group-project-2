Vision

The vision of the Asian Gamesis to unite the diverse nations of Asia through the power of sport, fostering a spirit of peace, friendship, and mutual understanding across the continent. The Games aim to celebrate Asia's rich cultural diversity while showcasing athletic excellence. Mision

The mission of the Paris 2024 Olympic Games is to deliver an inclusive, sustainable, and innovative event that embodies the Olympic values of excellence, friendship, and respect. Paris 2024 is committed to minimizing the environmental impact of the Games, with a strong focus on sustainability and reducing carbon emissions. The Games aim to be a celebration of diversity and accessibility, ensuring that people from all backgrounds and abilities can participate and enjoy the event. By leveraging cutting-edge technology and promoting cultural exchange, Paris 2024 seeks to create a memorable and engaging experience for athletes, spectators, and global audiences alike.
