Table Tennis Overview at Paris 2024 Olympics

Event Details

  • Event Dates: July 27 - August 10, 2024
  • Venue: Paris Expo Porte de Versailles, Paris

Overview

Table Tennis is a popular Olympic sport that features fast-paced matches requiring quick reflexes and strategic gameplay. The competition includes five events:

  • Men's Singles
  • Women's Singles
  • Men's Doubles
  • Women's Doubles
  • Mixed Doubles

Each match consists of the best of seven games, and players or teams need to win four games to secure the match. The sport is dominated by countries like China, Japan, and Germany, known for their skilled players and strong table tennis traditions.
