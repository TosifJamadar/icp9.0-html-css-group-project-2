Vision

The vision for the Paris 2024 Olympic Games is to deliver a more inclusive, sustainable, and accessible event that reflects the spirit of the modern world. The Games aim to inspire a new generation of athletes and spectators by embracing innovation, cultural diversity, and the Olympic values of excellence, friendship, and respect. Paris 2024 envisions creating a lasting legacy for the city and the broader global community by promoting environmental responsibility, social inclusion, and economic development. At its core, the vision is to deliver a Games that not only celebrate athletic excellence but also serve as a platform for social change.Sport has the power to change everything, improving education health, and social inclusion. Paris 2024 is all about harnessing the once-in-a-lifetime opportunity, as well as the enthusiasm and energy the Games will spark.

Mision

The mission of the Paris 2024 Olympic Games is to deliver an inclusive, sustainable, and innovative event that embodies the Olympic values of excellence, friendship, and respect.At Paris 2024, we believe that sport can change lives. Paris 2024 is committed to minimizing the environmental impact of the Games, with a strong focus on sustainability and reducing carbon emissions. The Games aim to be a celebration of diversity and accessibility, ensuring that people from all backgrounds and abilities can participate and enjoy the event. By leveraging cutting-edge technology and promoting cultural exchange, Paris 2024 seeks to create a memorable and engaging experience for athletes, spectators, and global audiences alike.Playing sport leads to healthier, more fulfilling lives. Sport encourages values that make societies more inclusive.
